Our Story

Marada Beauty Soap was created with a passion for celebrating natural beauty. Our mission is to enhance your skin’s authentic glow without bleaching or altering its natural tone, using the gentle power of nature to deliver radiant, healthy skin for everyone.

Crafted with pure, natural ingredients like whole tomato, carrot, and yogurt, Marada Beauty Soap contains no preservatives or artificial additives. This skin-friendly formula moisturizes deeply, foams richly, and promotes a natural, radiant glow, making it ideal for all ages and skin types. Proudly NAFDAC-approved, it ensures quality and safety you can trust.
